KAPPA FISH CURRY

Cassava or Tapioca is referred to as Kappa in Malayalam. Kappa is boiled to form a smooth texture and then eaten with Meen (Fish) Curry.

KERALA PUTTU

Puttu is a dish native to the Southern Indian state of Kerala, Tamil Nadu, parts of Karnataka, and Sri Lanka. Puttu means "portioned" in Tamil and Malayalam.

CHICKEN BIRIYANI

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices.

PATHIRI CHICKEN CURRY

Pathiri is a pancake made of rice flour. It is part of the local cuisine among the Mappilas of Malabar region in Kerala State of Southern India.

NOODLES

Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough which is either rolled flat and cut, stretched, or extruded, into long strips or strings.

VEG MEALS

Sadya is a meal of Kerala origin and of importance to all Malayalis, consisting of a variety of traditional vegetarian dishes usually served on a banana leaf in Kerala as lunch

MASALA DOSA

Masala dosa is a dish of South India. It is a type of dosa originating in the town of Udupi, Karnataka.

PAYASAM

Kheer, also known as payasam or payesh, is a pudding/porridge popular in the Indian subcontinent, usually made by boiling milk, sugar or jaggery, and rice.
